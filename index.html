Preparing for the Update

Before starting the update process, it’s important to prepare your device correctly:

  • Ensure your Instinct 2 Solar is charged to at least 50%.
  • Install or update the Garmin Connect app on your smartphone.
  • Connect to a strong Wi-Fi or data connection.
  • Make sure your watch is paired via Bluetooth to your phone.

How to Update Garmin Instinct 2 Solar

Option 1: Using Garmin Connect Mobile

  1. Open the Garmin Connect app on your phone.
  2. Make sure your Instinct 2 Solar is paired and connected.
  3. The app will check for available updates automatically.
  4. If an update is available, follow the prompts to install it.
  5. Stay close to your phone and keep the app open until the update completes.

Option 2: Using Garmin Express on Your Computer

  1. Download and install Garmin Express.
  2. Connect your Instinct 2 Solar to your computer via USB.
  3. Launch Garmin Express and add your device.
  4. If an update is found, click “Install” and wait for completion.
  5. Safely disconnect your watch when the update is complete.

Troubleshooting Update Problems

  • Update stuck: Restart your watch and phone, then retry through Garmin Connect.
  • Device won’t sync: Unpair and re-pair your watch in the Garmin Connect app.
  • No update found: Confirm you’re using the latest version of Garmin Connect or Express.
  • Watch unresponsive: Perform a soft reset by holding the top-left button until reboot.
